About Us

Welcome to Triple Threat Bullys! We are a XL American Bully dog kennel located in North, AL. Our kennel is home to several dogs who are all members of our family. Our mission is to provide high-quality care for all of our puppies and to help them find loving forever homes.


At Triple Threat Bullys, we specialize in XL American Bullys. Our dogs are all purebred and come from champion bloodlines. They are well-socialized and will have been raised in a loving environment from the start. We take great care in making sure our dogs are healthy, happy, and well-adjusted.


We offer dog breeding services for those who are interested in adding a XL American Bully puppy to their family. At Triple Threat Bullys, we believe that every dog deserves a loving and caring home. That's why we go above and beyond to ensure that our dogs are well-cared for and happy. We are dedicated to finding the perfect home for each of our puppies and take the time to get to know each potential buyer to ensure a successful match. Thank you for considering Triple Threat Bullys for all your dog needs. We are passionate about what we do and are committed to providing the best care possible for our dogs. If you're interested in purchasing one of our puppies, please don't hesitate to reach out. We'd love to hear from you.
